Gas Station Opens in City

Back to Hub

A gas station has opened in the south side of the city.

Just today a gas station has opened in the city, with '15 pumps', and '5 charging stations'.

The station also has a car wash, and a place to fill your tires with air, as well as plently of food and drink inside.

I'm very excited to see the traffic that this new store will bring to the city. A local man stated I think this will be good for our city.
